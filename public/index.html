On-Campus Start-up Incubator with Funding

he On-Campus Start-Up Incubator provides students access to resources to develop their start-up ideas. Events such as hackathons, industry masterclasses, participation in industry conferences and events, mentorship boot camps etc. ensure that ideas grow beyond a business plan and feasibility study to an actual start-up. The institute offers financial support of up to 1 cr annually.

Idea

Interested students develop an idea for a product or service and document it.

Pitch

Ideas are developed into a business plan and presented to an internal committee. If the idea has merit and is selected, it moves to the next stage.

Prototype Support

Students get support from faculty and industry mentors to build a prototype.

Incubation

At this stage, the idea matures into a business and takes anywhere between 3 to 6 months to develop its metrics and profitability.

Funding

ITM Business School has set up a seed fund of INR 10,000,000 to fund qualifying startups within the incubator. Angel investment and VC networking opportunities are provided, too.

Global Immersion Programs

In collaboration with international universities and corporate houses, ITM offers the Global Immersion Programs (GIP) at four locations for this program: UAE, Malaysia, Singapore, and Europe. Participating in the GIP is mandatory for the International Business students. For all other specializations it is optional. Students are encouraged to participate because the experience provides an opportunity to interact with global industry leaders and exposure to multicultural environments.
